December 11 to 17, 2023

About first steps:
  • Regarding machine learning, we've decided to work with small samples gradually to test the model. In other words, we will test the model in successive waves, handling between 3 to 5 files at a time. We will also need to address the issue of missing values, which will be a significant task.
    To begin our project, we have decided to divide the tasks among ourselves. Here are the initial points for investigation:

    1 - Identify the first documents to be used.
    2 - Write an article about machine learning algorithms, covering their advantages, disadvantages, application conditions, inputs, and outputs.
    3 - Research and write an article about dealing with missing values (NaN) in machine learning.
    4 - Find documentation and write an article about handling imbalanced data.
    5 - Investigate the computational power required to implement machine learning on big data.

List of things done:
