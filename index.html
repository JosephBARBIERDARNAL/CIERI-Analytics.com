Accelerate analysis, create tools and develop data processing methods for the social sciences

CIERI Analytics is a blog about identity, behavior and data science. We aim to develop underexplored methods and tools to analyze data in the social sciences. We believe that technical aspects of data analysis should not be a barrier to the development of new research questions. We also believe that data analysis should be reproducible and transparent.

Our current main project is the SHARE Survey. SHARE is a longitudinal survey of the population aged 50 or older in Europe with a focus on health, social and economic conditions. This survey has massive potential to answer important questions about the the European population since it provides a large amount of data.
